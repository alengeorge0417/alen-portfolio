About Me

I’m Alen, an audio engineer and musician based in India, driven by emotion, clarity, and detail. I’ve worked on a wide range of projects including radio production, film songs, independent music, and advertising. I’ve collaborated with names like Ranjin Raj, Swetha Lis Mathew, Jewel Mary, and Richbell Properties. My style is a fusion of crisp sound design, layered vocals, and emotional storytelling — whether behind the mic or behind the mix.
